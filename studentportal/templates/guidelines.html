#} {# General Instructions#} {#

#} {##} {#
    #} {#
  1. #} {# Registration on the SG/CW Portal
    #} {# The student must register their project on the SG/CW portal as per the format. Any information which#} {# could not be completely conveyed in the form must be uploaded as a 'Proposal' file in the upload files#} {# section of the project in this format. The registration for the same must be done before the add/drop date of#} {# the particular semester.#} {#
    2. #} {##} {#
  2. #} {# Proposal Approval and Registration on ERP
    #} {# After submitting the proposal, the student should wait for a mail accepting their proposal before#} {# starting the work. Once the proposal is accepted, the student must register Self Growth/Community Work#} {# credits on ERP (applicable for 2017 and later batches) by the last date of add/drop and can continue#} {# working on it during the upcoming semester until the semester ends.#} {#
    3. #} {##} {#
  3. #} {# Weekly Log Submission
    #} {# While carrying out the activity, a student has to maintain the log report for each day. The report#} {# should mention the details of work done each day (hourly report), and should be submitted (after getting#} {# it signed by the concerned supervisor) on weekly basis on the SG/CW portal.#} {#
    4. #} {##} {#
  4. #} {# Work Completion
    #} {# As soon as the activity is completed, a student has to obtain a completion certificate from the#} {# respective organization/NGO. The certificate should have the organization stamp and contact number of#} {# the person in-charge from the organization. Student is also expected to prepare a report and submit it#} {# on the mentioned date which will be one week before the Self Growth/Community work presentation in the#} {# format specified here.#} {#
    5. #} {#

#} {# Self Growth/Community Work Presentation#} {#

#} {##} {#
    #} {#
  1. The report should be submitted at least a week before the day of presentation which will be held in the#} {# beginning of the next semester.#} {#
    2. #} {#
  2. Together with the report, a completion certificate from the organization where he/she did the activity#} {# is to be submitted on the portal.#} {#
    3. #} {#
  3. The student also has to prepare a presentation (set of 8-10 slides)/poster.
    4. #} {#
  4. There will be a presentation every semester (during the first 2-3 weeks, as per the date specified in#} {# the calendar) wherein the student has to present the activity undertaken by him/her. Below is the#} {# semester wise presentation schedule that is to be followed:#} {#
      #} {#
    1. Work done in Monsoon Semester - Presentation to be done Winter Semester (as per the date#} {# specified in the calendar)#} {#
      2. #} {#
    2. Work done in Winter Semester - Presentation to be done Summer Term (as per the date specified in#} {# the calendar)#} {#
      3. #} {#
    3. Work done in Summer Term - Presentation to be done in Monsoon Semester (as per the date#} {# specified in the calendar)#} {#
      4. #} {#
    4. Note: In case a student fails to follow the above presentation schedule he/she has to start#} {# afresh, by submitting the proposal, taking approval and doing the work again.#} {#
      5. #} {#
    #} {#
    5. #} {#
  5. Students will also be required to submit the poster / presentation on the portal within 2 days of the#} {# presentation.#} {#
    6. #} {#
  6. The report, presentation and completion certificates, all these documents are mandatory for the student#} {# to be considered for evaluation.#} {#
    7. #} {#
  7. Based on the presentation student will be awarded “S-Satisfactory” or “X-Unsatisfactory”. In case of#} {# failure to present the work in the subsequent semester, student will be awarded “X-Unsatisfactory”#} {# grade.#} {#
    8. #} {#
  8. The earned credits will be mentioned in the final transcript.
    9. #} {##} {#

#} {# Report Structure for SG/CW Activities#} {#

#} {# Clearly no single report structure will suit all types of work. However, some things are essential:#} {#
    #} {#
  1. #} {# Name, Roll Number#} {#
    2. #} {#
  2. Title of the activity, and start date and end date
    3. #} {#
  3. Details about the Organization: Briefly explain the working of Organization - what do they do? How do#} {# they sustain themselves (in terms of funding)? What is the organizational structure? How many employees?#} {# What are the existing technologies currently in place? etc.#} {#
    4. #} {#
  4. Goals and achievements – In detail describe what the Organization/group wanted you to achieve during the#} {# semester (if a group is formed, this will be one common document) and what you have been able to#} {# achieve.#} {#
    5. #} {#
  5. Plan and adherence – how you planned to achieve the goal. This should be developed based on discussion#} {# with the Organization (again for the group, this will be one common document) and how was your adherence#} {# to the plan all through the activity.#} {#
    6. #} {#
  6. Weekly log – for each week, how many hours were spent, and what specific things were done in that week.#} {# If you had the log on a page include the pictures of your log in your report itself. Do not create a#} {# separate log file.#} {#
    7. #} {#
  7. Certificate from the organization: Include the certificate from the organization as a picture in your#} {# report.#} {#
    8. #} {#
  8. Team Members: List out names of other students who also did the SG/CW with you with the same#} {# Organization (for the group, list out all the group members).#} {#
    9. #} {#
  9. Scope of technology intervention: Based on your experience working with Organization and what you have#} {# learned at IIIT Delhi, briefly explain how IT can play a role in improving upon the existing activities.#} {# If your work involved some technology intervention, please explain how it will help the organization and#} {# how will it be sustained after you finish your activity.#} {#
    10. #} {#
  10. Personal account of experiences including what transformation it has brought in your thinking.
    11. #} {#

#} {# Frequently Asked Questions#} {#

#} {#
    #} {#
  1. #} {# When can I register for SG/CW? Only at the start of Summer/Monsoon/Winter Semester, or at any#} {# time?
    #} {# The registration dates for SG or CW work will be same as Add/Drop dates as per the Academic Calendar for#} {# Summer/Monsoon/Winter. No registrations will be allowed after the last date of Add/Drop as per the#} {# calendar.
    #} {# The students of 2017 batch onwards have to register on ERP as well as SG/CW portal. The students of#} {# batches earlier then 2017, have to register on the portal. The proposal approval status will be#} {# reflected on SG/CW portal within a week’s time.#} {#
    2. #} {#
  2. #} {# Can I split my 80/90 hours into 40/50 each and can I do 1 credit of work in one semester and#} {# the other 1 credit in next semester?
    #} {# Yes, you can split your work.#} {#
    3. #} {#
  3. #} {# Is it necessary to give presentation right after the semester following the completion of#} {# SG/CW work?
    #} {# Poster presentation dates are notified in the Academic Calendar of each semester. It is mandatory to#} {# present your work in the subsequent semester.#} {#
    4. #} {#
  4. #} {# Are Powerpoint presentations / slides acceptable during presentation?
    #} {# No. You need to make a poster on chart paper or you can take printouts and pin them on the board. The#} {# idea is to nicely present your work done. We don’t expect a fancy poster, but your presentation should#} {# be informative and professional.#} {#
    5. #} {#
  5. #} {# Can I skip presentations?
    #} {# No. It’s mandatory to present, based on which final evaluation is done. Without the presentation, your#} {# SG/CW credit requirements cannot be fulfilled.#} {#
    6. #} {#
  6. #} {# I am not able to get a stamp on my certificate from organization.
    #} {# In special cases only, we allow a student to request the organization to write a formal mail validating#} {# student’s work and student is then supposed to forward the same to selfgrowth@iiitd.ac.in or communitywork@iiitd.ac.in (according to the activity),#} {# faculty in-charge and Academic Officer in-charge.#} {#
    7. #} {#
  7. #} {# Can I work for an Organization outside Delhi?
    #} {# Yes.#} {#
    8. #} {#
  8. #} {# Can I do SG or CW in Monsoon/Winter or is it only permitted in the Summer session?
    #} {# There are no restrictions regarding the scheduling of SG or CW work. If you can manage the workload#} {# during the regular semester, you may choose to do so. Your logs are verified against 80/90 or 40/50#} {# hours requirement for 2 or 1 credits respectively.#} {#
    9. #} {#
  9. #} {# After approval of proposal, is there a time limit within which I need to present my SG or CW#} {# work?
    #} {# Please see Q3 in this FAQ.#} {#
    10. #} {#
  10. #} {# When is the latest semester when I can present my SG or CW work?
    #} {# If you wish to graduate in June, you should have presented your work in the preceding Winter semester#} {# (i.e., your 8th semester). Therefore, you need to plan accordingly and submit your SG/CW proposal at the#} {# commencement of your 7th semester. There will be only one presentation in a semester, typically#} {# scheduled in the first month (as notified in the Academic Calendar).#} {#
    11. #} {#
